Our Mission

SmartPark offers its services for anyone wishing to enjoy a convenient and free parking. The big problem of the current century is finding parking in major cities. The system offers to share parking spaces that will be released in the near future and publishes it in real time to users who are looking for parking. Each one can reserve parking on the click from the smartphone and save valuable time while significantly lowering parking costs.
