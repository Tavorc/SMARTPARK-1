Reporting
To report click the hamburger on the side, select the OUT and enter the exact details - time, location, size and other notes. Then click the send button. The system will store parking information and offer it to other users. If another user Use the parking you have published and confirm that the details were indeed correct, you can earn a Smarties!

Searching
To search for parking click the hamburger on the side, select IN and enter the exact details - time, location, radius of search and other comments
Then click the send button. The system will take you to a new screen where you will be presented with all available parking at your request.
You can reserve parking by clicking on the same parking space, which will not be shown to other users. When the time comes to park the system, you will be sent a disclaimer and verify that the report has helped you.
