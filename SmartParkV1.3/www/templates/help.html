POST SMART[PARK]

You can either click on the side menu button on top or click 'OUT' from the home screen. Please fill out all relevant parking details souch as date & time, location, size and other notes. When done click 'DONE'. The system will store the parking information and it will be available to other users who interested in it. If someone will catch the SMARTPARK you have published and confirm that the details were indeed correct, you'd earn a SMARTIES!

CATCH SMART[PARK]

You can either click on the side menu button on top or click 'IN' from the home screen. Please fill out all relevant parking details souch as date & time, location and distance. You can filter your search by size or handicapped parking. When done click 'DONE'. The system will take you to a new screen where you will be presented with all available parking at your request.
You can reserve parking by clicking on the it. When the time comes to park, the system will sent a disclaimer and verify the SMARTPARK. In order to apply the search you'd pay 1 SMARTIES!
